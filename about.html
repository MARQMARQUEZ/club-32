Our guiding principles

Vision & Purpose

01

Vision

A premier platform for consumer goods industry professionals to connect and drive positive change — on the course, in the clubhouse, and in the conversations that follow a round well played.

We believe that exceptional golf and mutual respect create the trust that turns introductions into lasting partnerships, and that those relationships can help shape a more thoughtful, resilient consumer goods landscape.

02

Purpose

To create a community of like-minded industry professionals — leaders who inspire, innovate, and give back to society — united by shared standards and a genuine enjoyment of the game.

Club32 exists to deepen those bonds through structured play, an inclusive code of conduct, and initiatives such as our annual Charity Day, so membership means contribution and camaraderie as much as competition.

Binding standards

Code of Conduct

These five principles are the standard for membership. They apply on the course, at every club event, and whenever you represent Club32. There is no separate “social” rulebook — this is it.

01

Respect

All members are expected to treat fellow members, guests, club staff, and course personnel with courtesy and dignity. Behaviour that demeans, intimidates, or disrespects others will not be tolerated. This includes respectful discourse during disputes or disagreements.

02

Integrity

Members must uphold the highest standards of honesty and ethical conduct. This applies to scorekeeping, business dealings discussed at club events, and all interactions within the Club32 community. Any form of cheating or misrepresentation is grounds for review by the Executive Committee.

03

Professionalism

Members represent not only themselves but also Club32 and the broader consumer goods industry. Behaviour that could bring the club into disrepute — whether on the course, at club functions, or in any public forum — is strictly prohibited.

04

Inclusivity

Club32 is committed to maintaining a welcoming environment for all members regardless of gender, race, nationality, religion, or background. Discrimination or exclusionary behaviour of any kind will not be tolerated, and all members are expected to champion a culture of belonging.

05

Accountability

Members are accountable for their actions and commitments — including timely payment of fees, attendance obligations where applicable, and adherence to this Code. The Executive Committee reserves the right to address breaches formally, including suspension or removal from the club.
