Who We Are

A Private Club for Industry Leaders

Club 32 is an exclusive, private golf club designed specifically for professionals within the Consumer Goods Industry — spanning Retail, Wholesale, Pharmacy, Liquor, Pet Trade, and affiliated service providers.

Our membership is intentionally limited to just 32 fully paid-up members, ensuring that every interaction is meaningful, every event is intimate, and every round of golf is an opportunity to strengthen the bonds that drive our industry forward.

Why Join Us

More than a golf club — a community of industry leaders who inspire, innovate, and give back.

Networking

Build meaningful relationships with senior professionals across the consumer goods landscape. Every round opens doors to new partnerships, collaborations, and opportunities that transcend the golf course.

World-Class Golf

Play at premier courses in a well-organised, competitive yet collegial environment. Our structured tournaments are thoughtfully curated to deliver an exceptional golfing experience for players of all handicaps.

Community

We believe in giving back. Club 32 actively supports charitable initiatives, including our annual Charity Day, reinforcing our commitment to making a positive impact beyond the fairway.
