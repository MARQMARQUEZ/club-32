Eligibility

Who Can Join

Club32 is open to professionals across the consumer goods industry value chain.

Retail & Wholesale

Professionals operating within retail chains, independent retailers, wholesale distributors, and related trade organisations are warmly welcomed as members of Club32.

Pharmacy & Liquor

Representatives from the pharmacy sector and liquor industry — including manufacturers, distributors, and retailers — are eligible to apply for membership.

Pet Trade & Services

Pet trade professionals and service providers who support the consumer goods industry — including logistics, marketing, technology, and consulting firms — are also eligible.

Membership is strictly limited to 32 fully paid-up members. Spaces are extremely limited — we encourage interested professionals to apply early.

The Process

How to Apply

Joining Club32 is a straightforward process. Here's what to expect.

1

Apply in Writing

Submit a written application expressing your interest in joining Club32, including your professional background and industry sector.

2

Get Proposed

Your application must be proposed (seconded) by an existing Club32 member in good standing. This ensures cultural alignment with our values.

3

Committee Approval

The Executive Committee reviews all applications and will notify you of the outcome. Decisions are made in the best interest of the club community.

4

Pay Annual Fee

Upon approval, complete your membership by paying the annual fee via EFT to the club's Nedbank account. Membership is confirmed upon receipt of payment.
